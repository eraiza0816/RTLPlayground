### What works
The device is fully supported:
- ALL 2.5GBASE-T RJ45 ports work at 10/100/1000/2500 Mbps
- The SFP+ port supports 1G, 2.5G and 10G modules 
- LEDs work with the same indiciations as the OEM firmware
